Website content

From both a client experience and search engine visibility point of view it is important to continuously enhance your website.

  • A content rich website improves your website’s chance of showing up high in Google search results. A website with recently added content will always trump a similar site that has not changed since Google last indexed it.
  • New content provides you with an opportunity to invite your clients to visit your website.
  • You should always try to give returning visitors something new and thus provide a reason for them to keep returning.
