Your Corporate Business supports

Corporate business provides a number of excellent pension planning tools for you and your clients to use.

Corporate Business website

Corporate Business website

http://www.irishlife.ie/corporatebusiness/

  • Latest investment information
  • Monthly fund fact sheets
  • Weekly and monthly market commentaries
  • News section
  • A comprehensive download section.

Pension Planet Interactive

Pension Planet Interactive

http://www.pensionplanetinteractive.ie - This site gives you access to your pension plan information including:

  • Your account value
  • Your fund selection
  • Your transactions
  • Documents such as benefit statements and correspondence
  • Pension Choice - annuity quotes

Pension Prophet

Pension Prophet

http://www.pensionprophet.ie - This pension prediction calculator helps predict what level of retirement benefits your clients pension could provide based on certain assumptions.

My pension app

My Pension app

http://www.mypensionapp.ie - The first app of its kind in Ireland - this app allows your client to view:

  • Current fund value
  • Estimated fund value at retirement
  • Personalised investment literature and much more

Pension direct phone line

Pension direct phone line

1850 258 258 - This allows your client to check their pension fund value by phone, seven days a week any time between 8am and 10pm.
