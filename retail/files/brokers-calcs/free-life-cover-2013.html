Our learnings from the 2011 life cover giveaway

When it comes to protecting their family, taking the first step can be the hardest part for any parent. There are a number of reasons why families have no protection at all:

The free life insurance offer helped to overcome many of these barriers and created the opportunity for follow-on conversations. This led to many advisers converting up to 20% of cases. By providing free life insurance, we addressed these key barriers:

  • Creating opportunities to discuss protection needs and how affordable life insurance can be;

  • Clients who signed up demonstrated the need for getting protected against the unforeseen;

  • We know that this happens to young people and we’ve provided you with the statistics to back this up

  • Almost one in three of our death claims - 363 death claims (31%) - are on lives under the age of 55

  • Nearly one in three of the claims were due to sudden accidents, the second biggest cause of death by volume.*
