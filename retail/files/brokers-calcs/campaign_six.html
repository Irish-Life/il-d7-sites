Campaign 6: no or limited specified illness cover

The objective of the specified illness cover campaign is to target clients with no or limited specified illness cover. The campaign emphasises the need for SIC including facts in relation to the occurrence of serious illness in Ireland. The suggested accompanying brochure also clearly lays out the product benefits, which is best in market covering 51 illnesses.

 

 

 

 

  • Who: The client base targeted for this campaign are clients up to the age of 50 with no current specified illness cover. It also includes clients whose only SIC is part of their mortgage policy and will cover mortgage repayments only, in the case of serious illness.
  • We will provide you with all the components of the direct marketing campaign including the option to include booklets suited to the campaign.
  • A key benefit is the complete control you can enjoy in terms of customising the client base and letter.
  • Customising the database: You can easily add, edit or delete client records according to who you wish to include in the campaign and the size of campaign you wish to run
  • Customising the broker mail letter: You can easily customise the letter provided to include your own business details and logo and any other details you choose.
  • Including marketing material: To enhance the campaign you can choose to download or order booklets to accompany your chosen campaign. You will find the marketing materials link if you proceed with this campaign or on the big ideas homepage.
