Eligibility Questions


Ordinarily we would require you to have a child aged 13 or younger when you apply. However, for permanent tsb staff we are waiving the requirement to be a parent.

Getting your €25,000 Free Life Insurance is easy, once you meet the following criteria


  • You are aged 55 or younger
  • You are a resident of the Republic of Ireland
  • You consent to being kept informed in writing, by post, email or telephone about products, services or additional benefits that Irish Life believe might be of interest to you
