Eligibility Questions

Getting your €25,000 Free Life Insurance is easy, once you meet the following criteria


  • Your youngest child is aged 13 or younger when you apply
  • You are aged 55 or younger
  • You are resident of the Republic of Ireland
  • You are named on the Birth Certificate or Adoption Certificate of your child
  • You consent to being kept informed in writing, by post, email or telephone about products, services or additional benefits that Irish Life Group and GloHealth believe might be of interest to you
  • You have not had or do not currently have any form of malignant cancer, brain tumour, heart condition or heart surgery, stroke, multiple sclerosis, motor neurone disease, transplant, disorder of the kidney, liver or pancreas, you have not been an in-patient in the last 12 months(excluding childbirth) nor are you awaiting any medical results or future medical procedures, or you are currently unwell and planning to see a doctor in the near future.
