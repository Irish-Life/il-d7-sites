Securing Your Clients Legacy

With Inheritance Tax receipts up 33% since 2011, helping your clients protect their assets from inheritance Tax when they pass them on to their beneficiaries is a vital component of the Sales Advice process. It is an area where you as the Adviser can provide the solution through setting up a Life Assurance plan under Section 72.

To help you advise in this market, we have developed a series of supports including our market leading calculator which will work out your clients liability in a very simple way & is available 24/7.
