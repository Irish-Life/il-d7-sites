Living Benefits

In the first half of 2018, 3 out of 4 claims Irish Life paid were for living benefits Vs death claims.

3 OUT OF 4 CLAIMS PAID FOR LIVING BENEFITS

There’s a lot more to Life Insurance than paying claims when people die. In fact, the majority of the claims we pay are for Living Benefits – such as lump sums for specified illnesses, and regular payments if you can’t work due to accident or illness.
