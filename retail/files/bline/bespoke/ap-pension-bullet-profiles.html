1. Did you know?

Are you aware that Irish Life offer bullet commission payment options on our regular premium pension plans?

The bullet commission options are available on our Complete Solutions 1 contract for both Personal and Company Pension plans.

What are Bullet Commission Payments?

  • 4 equal commission amounts payable at the start of years 1,3,5,7 of each pension policy you write
  • Followed by 0.25% fund based renewal commission from the start of year 9
  • 7 Options are available through our Complete Solutions 1 (Personal & Company) Regular premium, pension contract, depending on the charging structure you require

2. How it works

The 7 bullet commission options available

Example of how it works

Based on a monthly premium of €500; Option 1 above would pay €450 commission at the start of years 1, 3, 5 & 7, totaling €1,800;.
Fund based renewal (FBR) commission of 0.25% would then kick in from the start of year 9 of the policy. The client would receive 100% net allocation with a 1% Annual Management Charge (assuming investment in the Consensus Fund or a fund with a similar charge).

3. The Opportunity

For your business there is less risk and a long term income stream

Our regular premium bullet profiles can help you increase the long term value of your business

  • On average your earnings can be as high as 90% more over the first 7 years of a plan than under a different commission option with an equivalent charging structure
  • All four bullet payments are paid up front at the start of years 1, 3, 5 & 7 providing a constant boost to your cash flow
  • Each bullet commission payment has only a 2 year earning period
  • We pay fund based renewal commission income of 0.25% from year 9 of the plan without any increase in charges to your client. As the fund value increases over time, so too will the amount of fund based renewal commission you receive.
