1. Calculate the Estate Value

Start by entering information to calculate the total value of the estate. This includes residences, investments, business assets, life insurance, and so forth.

2. Add Beneficiaries

Add up to four beneficiaries. Specify their relationship to the owner of the estate in question. Add some more information to determine their potential CAT liability.

3. Produce Report

Produce a printable report to give to your clients. It will contain a summary of all the information entered and the relevant CAT liabilities for each of the beneficiaries in the estate.
